About Upamanyu

A small, sharp digital agency from Nepal — built to ship work that performs.

We started Upamanyu Creation because the gap between what local brands could afford and what they actually needed to grow online had become too wide. Our answer: combine modern AI tooling with strong creative judgment, charge fairly, and ship faster than anyone expects.

The name Upamanyu comes from a story about a young seeker who refused to stop chasing the truth — even in the dark. We carry that spirit into every brief: keep going until the work earns its place.
